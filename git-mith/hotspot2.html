"RED DEVIL"






In 2008, Cristiano Ronaldo had a breakout year at Manchester United. He helped the club win the Premier League and the UEFA Champions League, scoring 42 goals in all competitions. Ronaldo’s performances earned him the Ballon d'Or, making him the world's best player. Known for his dribbling, powerful shots, and incredible heading ability, Ronaldo became a global superstar. His 31 goals in the Premier League were a standout achievement, and his influence was key to United’s success under Sir Alex Ferguson. It was a year that marked the peak of his early career.
